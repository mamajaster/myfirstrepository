About Me

One of my great passions in life is to bring awareness about children in foster care. Growing up, I knew that one day I wanted to foster children. After the loss of a pregnancy, my grief propelled me into my life as a foster mamma! Our family decided the perfect healing environment for fostering would be a farm. So we bought property and started the John 3:16 Farm! We have since adopted a sibling group of 3, which has ended our journey in fostering for the time being, as we have a very full house! But my mission is far from over. I am determined to bring awareness to the general public on the ever growing need of foster parents. Read more here on our mission at the John 3:16 Farm!
